Our Story

Crafted with Intention. Shared with Purpose

Because at Mannheim Craft Brewery, we don’t just brew beer — we craft purpose.

Rooted in German precision and driven by Indian curiosity, our beers celebrate quality, creativity, and community. From our flagship lagers to seasonal experiments, every pour is brewed with intention. Because for us, it’s not just about what’s in the glass — it’s about who you share it with.

Mannheim Craft Brewery was born from a shared belief — that great beer has the power to bring people together.

Founded by a team of friends turned visionaries, each with a different story but a common passion for craft, Mannheim was built to challenge convention and celebrate connection.

Rooted in the precision of German brewing and shaped by the energy of India’s evolving palate, we brew with intention — every pint reflecting our philosophy of quality without compromise.

Here, every sip, every smile, and every shared story is part of something larger — a movement rooted in purpose, passion, and people.
